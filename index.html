Seventh Edition Update

Thảo takes over Paris while Paulo upgrades heartbreak into robotics

Since Thảo stopped paying attention to Paulo, her public life has allegedly transformed into an international celebrity fashion circuit. She has become the hottest talk in fashion and celebrity magazines, moving from Paris runways to luxury hotel entrances and then to a suspiciously romantic Alpine walk.

Yesterday, she modeled high fashion at Paris Fashion Week in a dramatic black couture look. Later that evening, she was seen arriving at the George V Hotel in Paris with Jean Paul, the polished son of one of France’s wealthiest families. The day before, she was spotted in the German Alps with Moritz, now classified by the newsroom as one of Paulo’s recurring nemeses.

Paulo, observing these developments from the emotional disaster desk, has reportedly purchased a custom AI robot designed to look like Thảo. Sources say the logic was simple: if the real Thảo will not speak to him, perhaps the robot version will at least be nice in the kitchen.
